# Recent Advances in Machine Learning for Biology

Machine learning has revolutionized biological research over the past decade. In this post, we'll explore some of the most exciting developments and their implications for our work.

## Key Areas of Impact

### 1. Protein Structure Prediction

The breakthrough achievements of AlphaFold have demonstrated the power of deep learning in solving long-standing biological problems. Our team has been building on this foundation to address related challenges in protein-protein interactions.

### 2. Genomic Data Analysis

With the explosion of genomic data, traditional analysis methods struggle to keep pace. Machine learning approaches allow us to:

- Identify patterns in complex datasets
- Predict functional elements from sequence data
- Discover novel genetic variants associated with disease

### 3. Drug Discovery

ML models are accelerating the drug discovery pipeline by:

- Predicting drug-target interactions
- Optimizing molecular properties
- Identifying promising drug candidates earlier in development
